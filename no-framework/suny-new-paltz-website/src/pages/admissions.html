Admissions

Welcome to SUNY New Paltz Admissions

We are excited that you are considering SUNY New Paltz as your next step in education. Our admissions team is here to guide you through the process.

Admissions Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • Official high school transcripts
  • Standardized test scores (SAT/ACT)
  • Letters of recommendation
  • Personal statement

Important Dates

Make sure to keep track of these important dates:

  • Application Deadline: January 15
  • Decision Notification: March 1
  • Enrollment Deposit Deadline: May 1
